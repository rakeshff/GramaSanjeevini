# 📱 Project Overview

GramaSanjeevini is a modern Android application developed to solve one of the major healthcare challenges in rural and semi-urban regions — medicine accessibility and pharmacy coordination.

In many villages and remote locations, patients struggle to locate essential medicines quickly during emergencies. At the same time, medical stores often face issues such as:

* Medicine wastage due to expiry
* Poor stock visibility
* Lack of pharmacy coordination
* Difficulty locating life-saving drugs
* Absence of centralized medicine availability systems
* Manual inventory tracking
* Limited multilingual accessibility

GramaSanjeevini addresses these issues through a digital rural pharmacy network that connects villagers and pharmacists through a single platform.

The application enables:

✅ Villagers to search nearby medicines instantly
✅ Pharmacists to manage medicine inventory efficiently
✅ Near-expiry medicines to be sold at discounted prices
✅ Emergency medicines to be located faster
✅ Multi-shop medicine networking
✅ Multi-language accessibility for rural users

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Rural communities often face severe difficulties in obtaining medicines quickly during emergencies. Many villages lack:

* Centralized pharmacy systems
* Real-time medicine availability
* Inventory visibility
* Nearby pharmacy information
* Affordable medicine access

Additionally, medical shops frequently experience:

* Medicine expiry losses
* Overstocking
* Manual inventory errors
* Inefficient stock coordination

GramaSanjeevini was developed to digitally transform rural medicine accessibility and pharmacy networking.
